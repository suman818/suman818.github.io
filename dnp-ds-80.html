DNP DS80 Photo Printer

  • Ideal for studio settings, onsite printing, and photo retail solutions, our DS80 digital photo printer is widely deployed by professional photographers and photo retailers around the world to produce high-quality larger format images in beautiful full-color detail.
  • The DS80 can produce full-bleed 8”x 10” and 8”x 12” photos—all in a protective lamination layer that is resistant to fingerprints, dust, water, and fading. Due to a simple dye-sublimation transfer method and unique roll-fed design, the printer can be operated for extended continuous use with minimal downtime. The roll design also allows the printer to store a large volume of media for printing—the DS80 can produce 130 8”x 10” photos and 110 8”x12” photos before a media reload is required.
  • At only 12.6-inches wide by 14-inches deep by 6.6-inches high, the small footprint of the DS80 allows for the unit to be easily shipped or transported to any event and saves valuable workspace when utilized in photo lab applications
  • Additionally, the DS80 is built to the same dimensions as DNP’s award-winning DS40 printer for easy stacking to save space and provide additional print size options at high-volume printing locations.
  • Choice of matte or glossy print surfaces from the same media print pack
